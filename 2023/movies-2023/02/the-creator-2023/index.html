In the aftermath of a nuclear detonation in Los Angeles, The Creator plunges viewers into a world torn apart by war between humanity and artificial intelligence. The stakes are raised as the Creator, an elusive figure at the heart of the conflict, unveils a mysterious weapon with the potential to decisively end the war. To confront this existential threat, an ex-special forces agent, played by John David Washington, is recruited for a perilous mission: to hunt down and eliminate the Creator. One of the film's standout elements is its visionary direction by Gareth Edwards. Known for his work in visually ambitious films such as Monsters and Godzilla, Edwards brings his signature style to The Creator.
