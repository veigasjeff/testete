Synopsis: Hijack (2023) takes viewers on a gripping journey within a high-stakes and suspenseful thriller miniseries. Created by George Kay and Jim Field Smith and headlined by Idris Elba, the show made its debut on Apple TV+ on June 28, 2023. The series revolves around the harrowing ordeal of a hijacked plane during its seven-hour flight to London. Meanwhile, authorities on the ground are in a race against time, piecing together the puzzle and making critical decisions. In this tense and perilous situation, Idris Elba steps into the role of Sam Nelson, an accomplished corporate negotiator who applies his professional skills to the crisis, striving to save every life on board. Hijack received praise for its skillful storytelling, authentic portrayal of the complexities of a hijacking situation, and Elba's compelling performance. Notably, the series unfolds in real-time, with each episode representing one hour of the high-stakes drama, further intensifying the viewing experience. Critics largely commended the series, contributing to a Metacritic score of 66 out of 100. This critical acclaim was accompanied by commercial success, as Hijack became the second Apple TV+ production to earn a place on the Nielsen streaming rankings for original shows. In addition to Idris Elba, the series features an ensemble cast, including Archie Panjabi, Christine Adams, Max Beesley, Eve Myles, Neil Maskell, and Alex Ferns. Hijack (2023) is a must-see for enthusiasts of thrillers and suspenseful dramas.
